Crystal Collector Game

INSTRUCTIONS
You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will
add a specific amount of points to your total score.
You win the game by matching your total score to the random
number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.
The value of each crystal is hidden until clicked. Each new game, the crystals will have a new value.
Good Luck!
